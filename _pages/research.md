description: My research interests include nonmarket strategy, sustainability, international business, and strategic human capital. In the area of sustainability, I explore how firms strategically respond to a variety of institutional change or institutional pressures regarding sustainability, such as climate policy, green finance campaign, green credit policy, and physical climate change exposure. In the area of international business, I mainly explore how MNEs response to geopolitical conflicts through supply chain restructuring or relational governance. I am also interested in the effects of the natural environment or environmental regulations on labor markets and human capital outcomes. In my recent working projects, I explore the factors that drive the allocation of human capital both within and across organizations, and how organizations adjust human capital investment in response to external shocks.
